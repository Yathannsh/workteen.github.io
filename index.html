About Us

If you're an NGO, Foundation or any such agency, we can help you find interested volunteers and interns who'd want to help you. These volunteers can help you teach students, design posters, organise events, really, anything you need. Just let us know your requirements and we'll help you find people.

Our Team

Kunal Mishra

Kunal Mishra contributed extensively to the front-end design of this website. Most pages were made possible due to his tenacity. His ability of finding innovative workarounds contribute to the team's strength. For Kunal, WorkTeen is a portal to help his fellow classmates, most of whom are still pondering ideas for their CAS project. His capacity at WorkTeen involves maintaining and upgrading the user experience.

Aayush Chadha

An IBDP year 2 student, Aayush thought of WorkTeen after he completed his CAS requirements and sat down to reflect upon his experience. He then developed and designed the website with the other co-founders. When he isn’t making any changes to the code or prototyping new features, Aayush dabbles in the design of promotional content, write new posts for the WorkTeen Blog or seek new opportunities to put up

Yathannsh Kulshrestha

Yathannsh is an IBDP year 1 student, who can be identified as the Chief Technology Head of the website. Frustrated seeing his seniors start non-profits impaired with morals and reasons just for IB CAS hours made him passionate about WorkTeen's cause. His significant experience in multiple web technologies contributes towards enhancing the user experience and building new features. He is also responsible for the website's expansion and image in Jaipur.

Abhijoy Nandi

Abhijoy is an IBDP year 2 student, who can be identified as the head of communications and business at WorkTeen. His eye for design and knowledge gained as a student of Visual Art has been useful in defining many elements of the user interface. His role includes contacting various corporates to find internship opportunities and at the same time, expanding WorkTeen’s reach by utilising his extensive social circle.
